Add Presearch Bookmarklet

To enhance your browsing experience with Presearch, follow these steps to add our bookmarklet:

If you encounter issues, ensure you have copied the entire code and pasted it correctly in the bookmark URL field.

Manual Steps

1. Open Safari on your iPhone.

2. Tap the Share button and select Add Bookmark.

3. Tap Save to add the bookmark.

4. Go to Bookmarks and find the newly created bookmark.

5. Tap Edit and replace the URL with the copied link.

6. Tap Save to finalize.
